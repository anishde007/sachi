ABOUT

Dr. Sachinandan De serves as National Professor in the Animal Biotechnology Division at the National Dairy Research Institute, Karnal, Haryana. His distinguished career spans over 30 years of groundbreaking research, education, and innovation in the field of animal biotechnology.

His research encompasses molecular immunology, animal genomics, DNA and PCR-based genomic technologies, ruminant immunology, and antimicrobial resistance. He has published over 168 peer-reviewed international publications and has been granted three Indian patents for innovative biotechnological solutions.

Dr. De has developed five PCR and DNA-based technologies for dairy and animal sciences, streamlined PCR techniques for mutation identification, and pioneered simple DNA isolation processes for genomic research. His work on antimicrobial resistance has contributed significantly to understanding bacterial AMR pathogen transmission through the food chain.
